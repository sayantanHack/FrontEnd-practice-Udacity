Elephant

Elephant is very quite animal.Elephants are the largest land animals in the world.
The largest elephant on record was an adult male African elephant. It weighed about 24,000
pounds and was 13 feet tall at the shoulder! Elephants can live to be over 70 years old.

Scientific Name: Loxodonta
Food: Vegeterian
Weight: 5000-6000 KG
Height: 2.7-3.3 Meter

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They have
characteristic long noses, or trunks; large, floppy ears; and wide, thick legs.
There are two species of elephant. The Asian elephant and the African elephant live on separate continents and have many unique features.
